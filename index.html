Slow-Roasted Leg of Lamb

Fall-off-the-bone tender, deeply savory, and worthy of any occasion. Low and slow is the secret.

Method

Notes & Tips

Temperature Guide

145°F = medium (slightly pink, juicy) · 160°F = medium-well · 190°F+ = braised, pull-apart tender

Wine Pairing

Serve whatever red you cooked with — Syrah, Merlot, or a bold Côtes du Rhône.

Make It Ahead

The lamb actually gets better the next day. Reheat covered at 300°F with a splash of stock.

Mint Gremolata

Combine ½ cup chopped fresh mint, 1 minced garlic clove, lemon zest, and a pinch of salt. Scatter over carved lamb before serving.
